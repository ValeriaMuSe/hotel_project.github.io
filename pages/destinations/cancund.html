About Cancún

Cancun is a city in Mexico located on the Yucatan peninsula bordering the Caribbean Sea. Cancun is also a popular destination for students during the university vacation period in the spring, which is known for its beaches, numerous resorts, and nightlife. It is made up of 2 distinct areas: the more traditional downtown area and the Hotel Zone, the coastal strip with high-rise hotels, nightclubs, shops, and restaurants.
