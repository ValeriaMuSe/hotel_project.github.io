About Bahamas

The Bahamas are an archipelago characterized by its corals in the Atlantic Ocean. Among its more than 700 islands and keys, there are some that are deserted and others that have very popular tourist complexes. Among its more than 700 islands and cays, they have very popular tourist complexes, such as the Grand Bahama, and Paradise Island, home to many high-level hotels. Some of its deep and surface diving sites include the enormous Andros coral reef. , Thunderball Grotto (featured in James Bond movies) and the Black Coral Gardens near Bimini.
