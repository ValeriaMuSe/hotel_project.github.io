Quinta Real Acapulco is a majestic hotel located in the Zona Diamante area of Acapulco, Guerrero on Paseo de la Quinta and offers spectacular, luxurious suites. Recognized as one of the most important and extraordinary hotels in Mexico since its inauguration in 1998, this luxury Acapulco hotel is an impressive five-story property beautifully situated on a 24-meter hillside that rises on the beach of Punta Diamante.
